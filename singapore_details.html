1. How to apply for a Singapore visa from India?

Applying for a Singapore visa from India is quite simple. It can be done through a government-authorised channel, either online via the Singapore Immigration & Checkpoints Authority or at an authorised visa application centre.

Here is what the visa application process looks like:

  • Step 1: Gather all the necessary documents, including your passport, photograph, and financial proof.
  • Step 2: Fill out the Singapore visa application form carefully and upload the requested documents.
  • Step 3: Submit your application and pay the visa fee as instructed.
  • Step 4: Wait for the visa processing, which usually takes a few working days.
  • Step 5: Once approved, you will receive your Singapore e-visa digitally. You are advised to keep a physical copy when travelling to the country.
2.Do I need to submit my passport for the Singapore visa process?

Yes, you will need to submit your passport during the Singapore visa application process. This is necessary to verify your identity and issue an electronic visa. Once your visa is issued, the passport will be safely returned to you.

3.How will I receive my approved Singapore visa?

Once your Singapore visa application is approved, you will receive the visa electronically via email. Here's what you should do:

Print a Copy:Having a physical copy is always safe as it helps against any digital failures.

Save a digital version:Keep a digital copy on your smartphone or tablet for easy access.

Before you travel, double-check all the details on your visa, such as your name and travel dates, to ensure everything is correct.
